Local authorities and multi-academy trusts (MATs)

You can sign in as a local authority or MAT to manage your RPA membership and that of your schools. Signing in as a local authority or MAT will enable you to see all of the schools linked to your organisation. You can sign these schools up or download their certificates from your account. Trusts can also access their trust-level certificate.

How to create a local authority or MAT account

To create an account for your local authority or MAT:

  1. Log in to DfE sign-in
  2. Select the 'Organisations' tab
  3. Under Related actions, select 'Request access to an organisation'
  4. Enter the name of your local authority or trust
  5. Select your organisation from the list and select Confirm
  6. Once you've received email confirmation that this has been approved, log back in to DfE sign-in
  7. Under Related actions, select 'Add services to my account'
  8. Select your trust organisation
  9. Choose RPA service
Signing in as a local authority or MAT does not commit you to signing up all of your schools to RPA membership

Contact us if you have any questions or problems signing up.
