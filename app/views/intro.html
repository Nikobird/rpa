DfE sign-in

You need to have an account with the DfE sign-in service to access the form. If you have not used DfE sign-in before, you will need to create a DfE sign-in account that is linked to your organisational setting.

Multi-academy trusts (MATs)

If you're a multi-academy trust (MAT), sign in as a MAT to get your trust and individual academy certificates.

To create an account for your MAT:

  1. Log in to DfE sign-in
  2. Select 'organisations'
  3. Select 'request access to an organisation'
  4. Enter the name of your trust

Contact us if you have any questions or problems signing up.

Signing in as a MAT does not commit you to signing up all of your schools to RPA membership
