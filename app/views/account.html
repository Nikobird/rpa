What type of RPA account would you like to access?


If you need to set up an RPA account for an academy, local-authority maintained school, or pupil referral unit:

    1. Make sure you have a DfE sign-in account
    • Go to the DfE Sign-in page (opens in new tab) and select ‘Create a DfE Sign-in account’.
    • Follow the steps in this process to create an account.
    2. Add your school to your account
    • Sign in to your DfE account.
    • Select ‘Request access to an organisation’.
    • Follow the steps in this process to submit your request.
    3. Wait for approval
    • Someone from your organisation (called an approver) will need to confirm your request.
    4. Add RPA to your services
    • Go to ‘Your services’ and select ‘See my approvers’.
    • Follow the steps in this process to add ‘Risk protection arrangement (RPA)’.
    • Once added, ‘Risk protection arrangement (RPA)’ will appear under ‘Your services’.Sign back in to your DfE account and select ‘Add services to my account’.
This allows you to view all the schools within your organisation. 

If you need to set up an RPA account for a local authority or multi-academy trust:

    1. Create a DfE Sign-in account
    • To create an account, go to the DfE Sign-in page (opens in new tab) and select ‘Create a DfE Sign-in account’.
    • Follow the steps in this process to create an account.
    2. Add your local authority or MAT
    • Sign in to your DfE account and select the ‘Organisations’ tab.
    • Under the ‘Related actions’, select ‘Request access to an organisation’.
    • Search for your local authority or trust and select ‘Confirm’.
    3. Wait for confirmation
    • You’ll receive an email once your organisation has been added.
    4. Add RPA to your services
    • Sign back in to your DfE account and select ‘Add services to my account’.
    • Select ‘Risk protection arrangement (RPA) service’.
