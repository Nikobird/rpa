Do you know that you can sign in with a local authority or MAT account?

Signing in as local authority or MAT enables you to see all of the schools linked to your organisation. You can:

  • sign up your schools to RPA
  • download your schools' certificates
  • download your trust-level certificate (MATs)

How to create a local authority or MAT account

To create an account for your local authority or MAT:

  1. Log in to DfE sign-in
  2. Select the 'Organisations' tab
  3. Under Related actions, select 'Request access to an organisation'
  4. Enter the name of your local authority or trust
  5. Select your organisation from the list and select Confirm
  6. Once you've received email confirmation that this has been approved, log back in to DfE sign-in
  7. Under Related actions, select 'Add services to my account'
  8. Select your trust organisation
  9. Choose RPA service
