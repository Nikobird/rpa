Enter start date

The start date must be no earlier than 3 months before today's date. If you have a commercial insurance policy in place, the date must be the day after this expires. For example, if your current insurance expires on 31 March, the RPA start date must be 1 April. This will ensure continuous cover and avoid any overlap. Cover will start from the date you enter, and payments will be deducted from your funding allocation from this date.
