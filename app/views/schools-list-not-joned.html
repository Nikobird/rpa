Select the rules that apply to your settings

It's important that you select the appropriate membership rules (opens in new tab) for your setting as this will determine the cover that you receive. Visit Get information about schools (opens in new tab) to see your school type and religious character. If your setting is a church academy (academy occupies property which is made available by a Diocese or other Church trustees), select 'Church academy rules'.

Enter start date

The start date must be no earlier than 3 months before today's date. If you have a commercial insurance policy in place, the date must be the day after this expires. For example, if your current insurance expires on 31 March, the RPA start date must be 1 April. This will ensure continuous cover and avoid any overlap. Cover will start from the date you enter, and payments will be deducted from your funding allocation from this date.
