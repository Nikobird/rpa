Risk protection arrangement

If your school is classified as a public sector school, you can join the risk protection arrangement (RPA) using your DfE sign-in account. The RPA is an alternative to commercial insurance which could save time and money. Once you’ve opted in, your certificate will be sent to you by email. Deductions will be made directly from your funding allocation from the date that you join.

You can use this service to:

  • join the RPA
  • view and download your insurance certificate if your school is already a member of the RPA

Before you start

We recommend referring to the gov uk RPA guidance page to find out more about the costs and terms of the RPA and how to create a DfE Sign-in account.

If you already have commercial insurance in place, your RPA membership must start from the date that this expires. If you have any queries about joining the scheme or opting out of it, email RPACover@wtwco-gsp.com

If you're already a member, you can make a claim here.

You need to have an account with the DfE sign-in service to access the form. If you have not used DfE sign-in before you will need to create a DfE sign-in account that is linked to your organisational setting.
