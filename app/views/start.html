Risk protection arrangement

The risk protection arrangement (RPA) is an alternative to commercial insurance which could save time and money.

You can use this service to:

  • join the RPA (academies, local-authority-maintained schools and pupil referral units)
  • sign up your schools (local authorities and multi-academy trusts)
  • view and download your RPA certificates

We work with partner organisations for claims handling and risk management. As a member of the RPA you'll receive access to free risk management advice and support, including seminars and interactive workshops.

When you join the RPA, we'll send your certificate by email. The certificate includes details of how to make a claim. Deductions will be made directly from your funding from the date that you join.

Before you start

Visit the GOV.UK RPA guidance page to find out more about the costs and terms of the RPA and how to create a DfE Sign-in account.

You can join the RPA at any time during the year. If you already have commercial insurance in place, your RPA membership must start from the date that this expires. If you have any queries about joining the scheme or opting out of it, email RPACover@wtwco-gsp.com.

You need to have an account with the DfE sign-in service to access the form. If you have not used DfE sign-in before, you will need to create a DfE sign-in account that is linked to your organisational setting.
