School Resource Management Self-Assessment Checklist (SRMSAC)

All academy trusts that have an open academy on 31 December 2023 must submit a 2023 to 2024 checklist.

This checklist helps governing bodies and trust boards to ensure that:

  • they have appropriate financial management and governance arrangements
  • they are meeting the standards to achieve a good level of financial health and resource management
  • resources are used to support high quality teaching and the best education outcomes for pupils
Trustees should approve the information provided before you submit the form or review the information submitted at the next board meeting.

Before starting the checklist, we recommend referring to:

  • Academies Trust Handbook 2023 (opens in new tab)
  • 2022 to 2023 checklist support notes (opens in new tab)

The checklist support notes provide a full list of the questions and details of any changes that have been made since last year. The notes also contain guidance for each question.

The deadline for submission is 15 March 2024.
